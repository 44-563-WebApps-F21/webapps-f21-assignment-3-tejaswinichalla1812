Kerala

Kerala is a place of tropical beauty with palm trees, white sand beaches and eco-tourism are all big reasons to explore the region. Besides its famous backwaters, elegant houseboats and temple festivals and Kerala is also home to Thekkady, a tiger preserve which allows you to admire flora and fauna without cards.


Directions from Maryville to Kerala, India

  1. Get a ride to Kansas(MCI) Airport from Maryville
  2. Kansas to Kerala, India
    1. Take a flight from Kansas to Mumbai
    2. From Mumbai take a flight to Kochi
    3. From Kochi to Allepey
    4. walk to Fort Cochin ferry station(maybe 15 mins)
    5. Take the fort cochin to Ernakulam ferry( maybe 15 mins)
    6. walk to the Ernakulam bus station(maybe 20-30 mins)
    7. take the bus to Alleppey (may be around 1.5 to hours)
